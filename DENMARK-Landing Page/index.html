About the Event

Morpho Detection invites you to Aarhus- Denmark to witness the integration of two powerhouse brands - Morpho Detection and BEUMER Group. Please join us for a demonstration of the integration in BEUMER's CrisBag® Tote BHS of Morpho's CTX 9800 DSi, both approved by ECAC as meeting the Standard 3 requirements.

Live demonstration event will showcase:
  • the seamless integration between the CTX 9800 DSi and the CrisBag Tote
  • the combined system’s benefits, including the elimination of baggage mistracks, prevention of lost baggage and enhanced security
